01 — About

Where science meets story

I'm a data scientist who thrives at the intersection of disciplines. With a foundation in rigorous statistical thinking and a passion for uncovering patterns in complex systems, I've spent my career translating raw data into meaningful insights.

From modeling financial risk to decoding genomic sequences, from accelerating drug discovery to advancing academic research — each domain has sharpened a different facet of how I approach problems. I believe the most powerful insights emerge when you bring cross-domain thinking to the table.

I'm driven by curiosity, grounded in rigor, and always looking for the next question worth answering.
